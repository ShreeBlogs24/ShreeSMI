Increased Focus on Sustainable CMP Solutions – Manufacturers are developing low-waste, eco-friendly slurry formulations.

AI and Automation in CMP Processes – Smart monitoring and AI-driven slurry optimization enhance polishing efficiency.

Expansion of Semiconductor Manufacturing Facilities – Investments in foundries and fabs globally are boosting CMP slurry consumption.
